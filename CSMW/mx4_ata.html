About The Author

Hello! My name is Charles Daniel Wu Bundalian, I am a scholar of the Philippine Science High School. I was born as the eldest son of a family of five on February 28, 2008. I am an INTJ-T 5w6 who enjoys many things, from long talks about anything all the way to doing advanced chemistry for fun, you are more than welcome to message me on discord at CharlesDaniel#1201 I'd love to talk to you!

I have been drawing for 2 years now with art as a side hobby, and as you can tell from my style, my favorite manga genre is Shoujo manga. I love romance and all the sweet filler in between, despite being terribly single and hopeless romantic... My favorite manga's include Osake Wa Fuufu Ni Natte kara (Love is Love Is Like a Cocktail), Otokonoko Zuma (My Wife Is a Man), Wotakoi: Love Is Hard for Otaku, and A.I. Love You. Anything sweet lovey-dovey is all you need to get me hooked!

When it comes to web design I have no experience, so I apologize for my lack of proper coding skills and ask for your patience and to bear with me (I'm running on pure stock knowledge from art class!). But I really do hope this website helps those who aspire to learn to draw manga, even in the smallest ways, please enjoy!

-Charles
