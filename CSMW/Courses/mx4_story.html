Male Course

Before you head straight to drawing we must first learn the most important part that can make or break a pretty manga, the story! Depending on the genre it can widely vary, here we'll deliver a basic course that covers the general aspect. Let's learn to write interesting stories to keep the readers attracted to our art!

In this version of the course we will be covering the basic gist of a general manga story, no specifics or special genres like action, adventure, business and commerce, comedy, detective, drama, historical, horror, mystery, romance, science fiction and fantasy, erotica (hentai and ecchi), sports and games, or suspense. Just plain story basics!

Steps On Making a Good Story

  1. Identify your genre, setting, and characters
  2. Create a proper plot starting from opening
  3. Follow up with a call to adventure or a rising action
  4. Create a climax to top it off
  5. Follow it with a resolving action to calm down the story
  6. Make a lovely ending or repeat the cycle from step 3!

Best of luck making your interesting story mangaka!
