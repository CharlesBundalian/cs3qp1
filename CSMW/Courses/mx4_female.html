Female Course

Females often have more slim and curvy physiques and petite anatomy, perfect for any sweet shoujo romance story or a thrilling shonen action with a female lead! Here we'll learn the basic parts of female characters in manga, the anatomy of where they go, and how to draw them!

In this version of the course we will be covering the basic anatomy of the female head, just be sure to note that we wont be covering specifics like eyes or noses because like anatomy can be tweaked based on personal preference, that is what make your art style, it's the beauty of drawing manga!

Steps On Making a Face

  1. Draw a circle and jawline base
  2. Make markings for eyes, ears, nose, and mouth
  3. Add neck, hair, shading, details, etc. and enjoy!

Note: the ear start at the lower eye line and peak at the top eye line, and the nose starts at the bottom of the ear!
