Story Course

Let's learn how to write a manga story!

Before you head straight to drawing we must first learn the most important part that can make or break a pretty manga, the story! Depending on the genre it can widely vary, here we'll deliver a basic course that covers the general aspect. Let's learn to write interesting stories to keep the readers attracted to our art!

Male Course

Let's learn how to draw male characters!

Males often have more muscular physiques and broader anatomy, perfect for any shonen action genre or male lead / love interest for a lovely shoujo romance story! Here we'll learn the basic parts of male characters in manga, the anatomy of where they go, and how to draw them!

Female Course

Let's learn how to draw female characters!

Females often have more slim and curvy physiques and petite anatomy, perfect for any sweet shoujo romance story or a thrilling shonen action with a female lead! Here we'll learn the basic parts of female characters in manga, the anatomy of where they go, and how to draw them!
